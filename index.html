EgoCross

A comprehensive benchmark across Surgery, Industry, Extreme Sports, and Animal Perspective. EgoCross comprises 798 clips and 957 QA pairs, supporting both CloseQA and OpenQA formats for fine‑grained evaluation.

Cross‑Domain Egocentric Video QA

About the Dataset

EgoCross evaluates cross‑domain generalization of multimodal large language models (MLLMs) for egocentric video question answering. It spans four domains and 15 sub‑tasks across Identification, Localization, Prediction, and Counting.
